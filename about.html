About me

Front-end developer from Lille, France

Meticulous Web Developer with passion for responsive website design and a firm believer in the mobile-first approach by creating web and mobile applications.

I originally started my career in Tech as a Brand Ambassador for Samsung Electronics in Switzerland for 3 years. Passionate about the Tech but also the Travel industry, after moving to Japan I then dedicated my first 3 years to promoting the Tohoku area by collaborating with JTB Corporation and Japan National Tourism Organization.

Since 2019, as a Consultant I have been working closely with business global leaders (Amazon, Uber, Hasbro, Regus, McKinsey etc.) and also unicorn Start-ups entering Japan market (Wolt etc.).

Through the SheCodes bootcamp, I have had the opportunity to dive into the technical side of tech and got addicted to the exciting creative approach of coding by building my own websites and apps from concept to implementation.

On my free time I love to travel and share the beauty of Japan through photography and videography on social media. Videogames take also a big part of my life and I am currently working on my first indie game project on Unity.
